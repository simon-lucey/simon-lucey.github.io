Graduate Admissions

We welcome applications from highly motivated students interested in machine learning and computer vision research. Applicants should have strong mathematical and programming backgrounds and a demonstrated interest in research.

PhD Applicants

PhD applicants should have strong academic results, prior research experience, and clear research interests aligned with the group. Publications, research projects, or industry research experience are strong advantages.

Masters Applicants

Masters applicants should have strong undergraduate performance in relevant disciplines such as computer science, engineering, mathematics, or physics. Research-oriented Masters students may transition into PhD pathways.

Before You Apply

  • Read several recent papers from the group.
  • Review the research themes to ensure strong alignment.
  • Prepare a concise CV highlighting technical and research experience.
  • Gather academic transcripts.
  • Prepare links to code repositories or technical projects where relevant.

Application Process

  1. Email a short introduction describing your background and research interests.
  2. Attach a CV and academic transcripts.
  3. Include publications, theses, or research reports if available.
  4. Provide links to GitHub or technical portfolios where relevant.
  5. Shortlisted candidates may be invited for interview.

Key Dates

Graduate admissions follow University of Adelaide timelines. Prospective students are encouraged to make contact several months before official deadlines.
